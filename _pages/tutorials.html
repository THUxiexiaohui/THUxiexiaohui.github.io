--- title: "Modeling User Behavior for Vertical Search:
Images, Apps and Products" permalink: /tutorials --- User behavior modeling for vertical search has been investigated by different communities, in information retrieval, machine learning and cognitive science. A key aim of this tutorial is to bring these together and offer a unified perspective. In this page, we provide supporting materials (e.g., slides, code data samples and etc). We warmly welcome you to give suggestions and comments to our tutorial!
This tutorial is given by:
Xiaohui Xie , Jiaxin Mao and Yiqun Liu, Tsinghua University, China
Maarten de Rijke, University of Amsterdam & Ahold Delhaize, The Netherlands
